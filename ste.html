Stegosaurus

Stegosaurus is a well-known genus of herbivorous dinosaur that lived during the Late Jurassic period, around 155 to 150 million years ago. It is famous for its distinctive, large bony plates along its back and the spikes on its tail, which have made it one of the most recognizable dinosaurs in popular culture.

Facts About Stegosaurus

  • Paleontologists Once Thought Stegosaurus Had a Brain in Its Butt
  • Stegosaurus Had a Brain the Size of a Walnut
  • The Spiked Tail of Stegosaurus Is Called a 'Thagomizer'
  • Stegosaurus Supplemented Its Diet With Small Rocks
  • Stegosaurus Was One of the Earliest Dinosaurs to Evolve Cheeks
