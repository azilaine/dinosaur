Triceratops

large quadrupedal plant-eating ceratopsian dinosaur that had a frill of bone at the back of its skull and three prominent horns. Fossils of “three-horned face”

Facts About Triceratops

  • The Triceratops had three horns on its skull.
  • The Triceratops was a large four-legged animal that looks like a rhinoceros.
  • The Triceratops can be up to 9 meters long or 29.52 feet.
  • It weighed around 12 metric tons or 12,000 kilograms.
  • The Triceratops can run up to 32 kilometers per hour.
