Dracorex

Its name, "Dracorex," means "dragon king," inspired by the dragon-like appearance of its skull. It was a member of the ceratopsid family, related to the more famous Triceratops, but it is notable for having a unique, spiky, dragon-like skull without the characteristic horns and frills of other ceratopsids.

Facts About Dracorex

  • Was possibly an omnivore
  • Was 2 car lengths long
  • Weighed as much as a wolf
  • Its name means “dragon king”
  • Could run as fast as a coyote
