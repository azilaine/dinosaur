Ornithischian dinosaurs

A group of herbivorous dinosaurs characterized by a bird-like pelvic structure. The name "Ornithischia" means "bird-hipped," referring to the arrangement of the bones in their pelvis, which resembles that of modern birds. However, despite their name, Ornithischians are not directly related to birds, as birds evolved from another group of dinosaurs (the theropods).
