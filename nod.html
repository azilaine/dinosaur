Nodosaurus

Nodosaurus is an extinct genus of armored dinosaur that lived during the Late Cretaceous period, about 100 to 70 million years ago.

Facts About Nodosaurus

  • "Nodosaurus" means "knobbed lizard".
  • Nodosaurus lived between about 110 million years ago and 100 million years ago, during the Cretaceous period.
  • Nodosaurus lived in North America.
  • Nodosaurus was about 13 to 20 feet (4.0 to 6.1 meters) long.
  • Nodosaurus weighed about 1 ton.
