Edmontosaurus

Edmontosaurus is a genus of duck-billed dinosaur (hadrosaurid) that lived during the late Cretaceous period, approximately 73 to 65 million years ago. It was a large, herbivorous dinosaur that roamed what is now North America, particularly in the regions that are now the western United States and Canada.
